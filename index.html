Traffic

  • Traffic lights work, even when it rains
  • Extensive use of traffic circles
  • People park their cars on the side of the road
  • Parking in the city is expensive, use public transport

Costs of public transport

  • Bus costs between $1.85 - 3.15 per trip depending on zone
  • I spend about $130 per month on public transport
  • Travelling around the city is preferable by bus

Cars / Driving

  • You can use your South African drivers license initially.
  • Conversion to NZ Drivers requires an eye test.
  • Car insurance $50 p/m

Schooling

  • You start school when you turn 5
  • Schools are rated by a decile system
  • We paid $100 donation per year for our son's school
  • School uniforms seem expensive, $80-$90 per uniform

Daycare

  • Costs around $320+ per week
  • When child turn 3, goverment sponsors 20 hours a week
  • No nanny's, or at least it's outside of our affordability

Housing

  • Average house in Auckland is $1 Mil (R10 Mil)
  • Pay a 20% deposit for a bond (R2 Mil)
  • Houses sold on auction
  • Housing is out of the reach of many young families

Renting

  • View a property for 30 mins
  • 40-50 people turn up if it looks semi decent
  • You then have to "apply" to rent
  • 3 bedroom costs between $500 - $650 per week
  • 4 week deposit upfront

Common living expenses

  • Basic house hold insurance $30 per month
  • Internet $90 per month for basic uncapped
  • Food costs roughly double than SA
  • $20 prepaid for 1 Gig Mobile Data + Talk + SMS

Demographics of Software Community

  • Polish, French, Brazilian, South African, Indian, etc.
  • C#, Java, JavaScript, Delphi?
  • Predominantly male dominated but working at it

Challenges of Software Community

  • Microservices Craze - microservices solve everything :-)
  • Test automation
  • Agile is a thing, depending on where you work

Looking for work

  • Growing demand for software developers
  • Historically they like to interview in person
  • More companies are offering re-location plans, i.e. MYOB

Relocation before you leave

  • Direct economy flights, taxis and transfers for you and any immediate family relocating
  • Costs of any Visas or Work Permits
  • Travel Insurance for medical and emergency situations
  • A hotel stay for the night before you leave

Relocation when you arrive

  • Reasonable accommodation in a hotel or serviced apartment for 4-8 weeks
  • Car hire for up to 8 weeks
  • Reasonable costs associated with shipping personal effects
  • 5 days Paid Relocation Leave
  • Reasonable costs relating to the relocation of pets

Salary expectations

  • Senior developer around $90K - $130K per year
  • In Australia you get 9.5% on top of base for superanuation (pension)
  • In New Zealand you need residency before qualify for Kiwi Saver

Interview Process

  • Coding Problem
  • Technical Inteview
  • Cultural Interview
  • End to end took 2 weeks

VISA Process

  • I am NOT an immigration consultant!
  • Visa Process was quick
  • Watch out for the health checks
  • Costs for work visa was about $200 (I think)
  • Often the main applicant travels over first, family follows

Applying for Residency (part 1)

  • Points based system, just increased to 160 points
  • Points determined on education, skills shortage, work, experience
  • English language costs around R4000 per person
  • Degree certification costs around R8000

Applying for Residency (part 2)

  • Letters for work experience should be signed and have hours per week listed

Getting Residency

  • Most people only apply once they are in New Zealand
  • Costs $550 to put yourself in the pool
  • Once you are selected you pay $2500 application fee
  • Takes from 3 - 6 months after applying that you get a response
